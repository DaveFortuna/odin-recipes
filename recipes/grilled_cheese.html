Grilled Cheese

Here is a list of ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Combining the ingredients together

  1. Heat up a pan on the stove top to medium heat.
  2. Apply a thick layer of butter to one side of 2 pieces of bread
  3. Place bread into pan buttered side down, and apply a layer of cheese on top of the bread.
  4. Place the second piece of bread on top of cheese layer, buttered side up.
  5. Cook until bottom is toasted
  6. Flip and repeat step 5, than plate and eat.
